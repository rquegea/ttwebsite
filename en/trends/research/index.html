Frequently asked questions

What research methodologies do you use?

We combine quantitative methods (online surveys, panels, statistical analysis) with qualitative approaches (focus groups, in-depth interviews, ethnography). Methodology is always tailored to the objective.

How long does a market study take?

A basic quantitative study can be completed in 3-4 weeks. More complex projects with a qualitative phase and benchmarking require 6-10 weeks. We always agree on timelines before starting.

What is coolhunting and how can it help my brand?

Coolhunting is the early detection of cultural, aesthetic and consumer trends before they go mainstream. It allows your brand to get ahead, innovate and position itself as a leader rather than a follower.

Which industries do you have experience in?

We've conducted studies for FMCG, banking, education, retail, luxury and technology, among others. Our team adapts tools and frameworks to each industry's context.
