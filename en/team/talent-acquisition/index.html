Frequently asked questions

What does a talent acquisition process with T&T include?

It includes role definition, active and passive candidate sourcing, competency-based interviews, cultural fit assessment, shortlist presentation and onboarding support.

Which sectors does T&T recruit for?

We specialise in marketing, communications, creative, technology and general management profiles. We work with organisations across all industries seeking talent in these disciplines.

What is employer branding and why does it matter?

Employer branding is the strategy that positions your organisation as a destination for top talent. A strong employer brand attracts better candidates, reduces hiring costs and improves retention.

How long does a typical selection process take?

It depends on the role. A standard process is completed in 4–6 weeks. For senior or executive search positions, the typical timeframe is 8–12 weeks.
