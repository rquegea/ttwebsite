Frequently asked questions

What types of corporate training does T&T offer?

We offer leadership programmes, digital upskilling and reskilling, creativity and innovation workshops, masterclasses and in-company training tailored to each organisation's specific needs.

Are training programmes delivered in person or online?

We design training in face-to-face, virtual and blended formats. The delivery method adapts to the company's culture, team distribution and learning objectives.

How do you measure training impact?

Every programme includes clear KPIs defined before launch. We measure satisfaction, knowledge acquisition, on-the-job application and, where possible, impact on business metrics.

Can training content be customised?

Always. Every programme is designed from scratch based on the sector, corporate culture, participant level and the organisation's strategic objectives.
