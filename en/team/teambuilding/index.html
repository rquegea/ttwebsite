Frequently asked questions

What types of team building activities does T&T offer?

We design indoor and outdoor dynamics, corporate retreats, gamification programmes, corporate escape rooms and collaborative experiences. Every activity is tailored to team size, objectives and company culture.

How many people can participate in a team building event?

From teams of 10 to events with over 500 participants. We adapt the mechanics and logistics to every format while maintaining the quality of the experience.

Does team building actually improve team performance?

Yes, when designed with clear objectives. Well-planned dynamics improve communication, reduce conflict, strengthen trust and increase sense of belonging. We measure impact with pre and post-activity surveys.

Can team building activities be run remotely?

Yes. We design virtual and hybrid dynamics that maintain high levels of interaction and engagement. These include interactive platforms, online gamification and professional facilitators.
