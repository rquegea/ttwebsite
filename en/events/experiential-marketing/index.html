Frequently asked questions

What is experiential marketing?

It's a discipline that creates direct encounters between brands and people through memorable experiences: activations in public spaces, pop-ups, immersive installations, sampling and street marketing that generate recall, engagement and viral content.

What types of brand activations does T&T design?

We design activations in shopping centres, festivals, urban spaces and strategic locations. Also pop-up stores, tech-driven immersive experiences, tastings, sampling campaigns and street marketing actions.

How do you measure the success of an activation?

We measure impact through KPIs such as footfall, interactions, samples distributed, leads captured, social media reach, press coverage and post-action brand recall.

How long does it take to prepare an activation?

A standard activation requires 4 to 8 weeks of preparation. More complex actions such as pop-up stores or tech-driven immersive experiences may need 2-3 months. We always work to tight timelines when the project demands it.
