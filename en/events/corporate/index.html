Frequently asked questions

What types of corporate events does T&T produce?

We produce annual conventions, sales kick-offs, townhalls, product launches, results presentations, galas, award ceremonies and corporate dinners. Every format is designed from scratch based on your communication objectives.

Do you handle hybrid and livestreamed events?

Yes. We design in-person, hybrid and fully virtual events. We manage end-to-end technical production: streaming platforms, multi-camera direction, real-time interactivity and audience metrics.

How far in advance should we start planning?

It depends on the scale. A large-format convention typically requires 3 to 6 months of planning. A townhall or smaller event can be organised in 4-6 weeks. We always recommend starting as early as possible.

Do you also manage logistics and technical production?

End to end. We handle venue selection, set design, lighting, sound, AV, catering, guest logistics, supplier coordination and technical direction on the day of the event.
