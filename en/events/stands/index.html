Frequently asked questions

What does the stand design and build service include?

It includes concept design, photorealistic renders, fabrication, transport, assembly, disassembly and storage. We also manage organiser paperwork, permits and coordination with the venue.

Do you work at international trade fairs?

Yes. We produce stands for fairs across Europe and international markets. We manage international transport logistics, insurance and compliance with each venue's local regulations.

Can stands be reused for multiple fairs?

Absolutely. We design modular and reusable systems that adapt to different fair formats and sizes, optimising your investment across multiple editions.

Do you integrate interactive technology into stands?

Yes. We incorporate interactive screens, augmented reality, presence sensors, lead capture systems and any technology that enhances the visitor experience and generates actionable data for your sales team.
