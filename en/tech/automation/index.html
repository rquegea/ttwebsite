Frequently asked questions

What automation tools do you use?

We work with leading platforms: HubSpot Workflows, Zapier, Make (Integromat), n8n and ActiveCampaign for marketing automation. We also build custom integrations when standard tools don't cover the need.

Can I automate without changing my current stack?

Yes. Most automations are built on top of your existing tools. You don't need to switch CRM or email platform. We connect what you already have and create flows that didn't exist before.

Does automation replace people?

No. Automation frees your team from repetitive tasks so they can focus on what truly matters: strategy, client relationships and complex decisions. Nobody loses their role — they gain time.

How quickly will I see results?

The first automations can be operational in 2-3 weeks. Results are immediate: fewer manual tasks, fewer errors, faster responses. The impact on business metrics typically shows within 1-2 months.
