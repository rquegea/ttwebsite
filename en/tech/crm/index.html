Frequently asked questions

Which CRM is best for my company?

It depends on your size, sector and digital maturity. HubSpot works well for growing companies that need integrated marketing and sales. Salesforce is ideal for complex operations with multiple teams. Zoho offers excellent value for money. We analyse your case before recommending.

How long does a CRM implementation take?

A basic setup can be operational in 4-6 weeks. Implementations involving data migration, integrations and advanced customisation typically take 2 to 4 months. We work in phases so your team can start using the system as soon as possible.

Can you migrate data from my current CRM?

Yes. We've migrated data between HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho, Pipedrive, spreadsheets and legacy systems. Every migration includes cleaning, deduplication and field mapping so you don't lose information or data quality.

Do you include team training?

Always. A perfect CRM is useless if your team doesn't use it. We design training sessions tailored to each role: sales, marketing, management and support. We also provide internal documentation for quick reference.
