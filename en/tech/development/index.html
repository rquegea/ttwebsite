Frequently asked questions

What technologies do you use for web development?

We work with the most robust frameworks on the market: React, Next.js, Vue, Nuxt for frontend; Node.js, Python, PHP and SQL/NoSQL databases for backend. The choice always depends on project requirements, not trends.

How long does a web development project take?

A corporate website is typically ready in 6-10 weeks. More complex platforms like e-commerce or native apps can take 3 to 6 months. We work in sprints with incremental deliveries so you see real progress from the start.

Do you build native or hybrid apps?

Both. Native apps in Swift (iOS) and Kotlin (Android) when performance is critical. Hybrid apps with React Native or Flutter when you need faster time-to-market and optimised budgets. We recommend the option that best fits your goals.

Do you provide post-launch maintenance?

Yes. All our projects include a post-launch support period. We also offer ongoing maintenance plans for updates, performance optimisation and feature evolution.
