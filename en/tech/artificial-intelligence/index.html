Frequently asked questions

Do I need an in-house technical team to implement AI?

No. Our team handles design, development and full integration. What you need is a deep understanding of your business — we bring the technology. After implementation, we train your team so they can operate independently.

What kind of results can I expect from AI?

It depends on the use case. A well-implemented chatbot can handle 60-80% of queries without human intervention. A predictive model can anticipate customer behaviour weeks in advance. We always define clear KPIs before starting.

Do you work with proprietary models or third-party APIs?

Both. We use APIs from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google and others when they fit. We also build fine-tuned models and custom RAG systems when the case demands it. The decision is based on cost, performance and data privacy.

Can AI integrate with my current CRM?

Yes. We integrate AI solutions with HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho and virtually any platform with an API. The goal is for AI to work within your existing tools, not replace them.
