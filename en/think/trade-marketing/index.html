Trade Marketing

Where the decision is made, we are there

The best product in the world loses if it's invisible on the shelf. We design point-of-sale strategies that turn presence into preference and visibility into real sales.

Let's talk

What we do

The point of sale is the last metre before the purchase. Every detail counts: location, visibility, message and experience.

Point-of-Sale Strategy

Shelf presence planning, space negotiation and shopper journey design that favours your brand.

Shopper Marketing

We understand the buyer. We design actions based on in-store behavioural data that accelerate the purchase decision.

Promotions & Activations

Promotional mechanics, sampling, contests and special activations designed to drive product trial and rotation.

Merchandising & POS Materials

Point-of-sale materials, display units, endcaps and visual elements that capture attention at the critical moment.

Category Management

Category analysis, assortment recommendations and planogram optimisation to maximise space performance.

Retail Activations

In-store experiences that connect brand and consumer. Pop-ups, tastings, demonstrations and point-of-sale events.

Why T&T

01

We understand the shopper

We don't design from the boardroom. We analyse real in-store behaviour data, traffic flows and purchase patterns for each channel.

02

Flawless execution

From concept to installation, we control every phase. In-house POS production, logistics coordination and on-site supervision.

03

Results at the checkout

We measure impact where it matters: at the till. Rotation uplift, sales increases and ROI for every activation, fully documented.
