Frequently asked questions

What does your communications service include?

Communications strategy, media relations, press releases, crisis management, SEO/GEO positioning, paid media (SEM, social ads, programmatic), conventional media planning and content marketing.

Do you handle SEO and paid media?

Yes. We manage organic positioning (technical SEO, content, link building) and paid campaigns (Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads, programmatic). Always with clear attribution models.

Do you manage media buying?

Yes. We plan and buy across TV, radio, outdoor, press and digital. We negotiate directly with media owners and agencies to optimise investment.

What is GEO and why should I care?

GEO is geolocation-based positioning. If your business has a physical presence or serves specific areas, GEO ensures you appear in local searches with high purchase intent.
